Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This application is developed by Demotass.Dev. Serenity Quotes respects your privacy and does not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name or real address).

Data Storage and Usage

The application manages two types of data:

1. Local Data (Preferences): Stored solely on your device and does not leave it:

  • Selected language.
  • Your favorite quotes.
  • Notification preferences.
  • Ad consent status.

2. Technical and Analytical Data (Firebase): Collected anonymously and sent to Google servers to support the app's operation and improvement (see next section).

Analytics and Statistics

This application uses Google Analytics for Firebase to help us understand how the app is used and improve its stability. This service may collect:

  • App instance identifiers.
  • Technical device information (model, Android version, language, country).
  • Interaction events (screens visited, buttons pressed).

This data is anonymous and is not used to personally identify you. For more information on how Google processes this data, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps.

Advertising

Serenity Quotes displays ads managed by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). If you accept personalized ads, your consent is automatically communicated to Google via the official User Messaging Platform (UMP). Google may use advertising identifiers according to its own privacy policies.

You can find more information here: Google Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Except for the technical services mentioned above (Google Analytics and AdMob), which operate under Google's strict privacy policies, Serenity Quotes does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Revoking Consent

You can modify or reset your ad preferences at any time through your Android device's privacy settings or by reinstalling the application to trigger the consent form again.

Personal Data

Serenity Quotes does not request or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise GPS locations, or any other sensitive personal data.

Contact

For any questions or requests regarding privacy, you can write to: demotass.dev@gmail.com
